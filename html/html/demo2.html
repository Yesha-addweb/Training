CORPORATE SPEAKING-CASSIE CAMPBELL-PASSCALL

Cassie Campbell-Passcall is available to speak at your corporate metting or function!

To find out more information on booking Cassie Campbell-Passcall for your upcoming event or corporate function, including available dates, fees and more, please Click here for more information.

Cassie Campbell-Passcall is a sought after corporate and key-note speaker, drawing on her accomplishments as the captain of both gold-medal winning Canadian Women's Olympic Hockey teams(2002 and 2006) to explore the topic of leadership, teamwork, determination and coming out of your comfort zone.

This combination of topics will assist with bringing a new and enhanced winning attitude to your executive team and your work force!

The result is a keynote speech that inspires, informs, and entertains.

Cassie Campbell-Passcall talks about her groundbreaking into the television business covering the NHL on CBC's Hockey night in Canada, as well as her experiences from her career as both an Olmpic athlete and now as someone who is on TV each Saturday night. Cassie Campbell-Passcall is a down to earth, passionate speaker that will motivate and leave a lasting impression on your staff.

Topics of Cassie Campbell-Passcall's corporate speaking:

  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Building a Successful team
  • Coming out of your comfort zone
  • Making a contribution
